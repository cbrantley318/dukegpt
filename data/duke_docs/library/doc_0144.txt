Overdrive & Libby
- Use OverDrive to access popular fiction and non-fiction eBooks and audiobooks that you can read on your computer, phone, tablet, or Kindle via the Libby App
- You can use the Libby app to read OverDrive eBooks and audiobooks. Have borrowing privileges at libraries other than Duke? You can add multiple libraries to your Libby account
Other resources
- NC LIVE: Access millions of eBooks through NC LIVE, North Carolina's statewide library cooperative
- HathiTrust Digital Library: Find millions of titles digitized from libraries around the world through HathiTrust, a partnership of academic & research institutions
Public libraries
The public libraries in the Research Triangle have robust OverDrive collections. Register for a card to access their collections online!
- Durham County Library — available to Durham County residents (details on registering)
- Orange County Public Library – available to residents of Orange County and other Triangle counties (details on registering)
- Chapel Hill Public Library – available to Orange County residents (details on registering)
- Wake County Public Libraries – available to Wake County residents (details on registering)
If your home address is elsewhere, check out your local library’s website!
