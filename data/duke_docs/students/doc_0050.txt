Hello, incoming students, and welcome to Graduate and Professional Student Services’ Summer Transition Series! This series will help you:
-
- INTERACT: Meet knowledgeable members of the Duke community
-
- COMMUNICATE: Ask questions directly to the hosts about topics relevant to Graduate & Professional students
-
- CONNECT: Access all sessions online and review recordings throughout the summer
REGISTRATION:
Registration is required for each webinar. If you are unable to participate during the scheduled time, recordings of each webinar will be posted 3-5 business days post-recording.
SUBMIT QUESTIONS BEFORE THE WEBINAR:
Not sure if you’ll be able to join live, but still have a question you want answered? You can submit questions before the webinar; a question submission form will be going live soon. Our moderators will do their best to answer all questions received during the appropriate webinar!
All sessions will be from 9-10 a.m. Eastern Time via Zoom webinar
