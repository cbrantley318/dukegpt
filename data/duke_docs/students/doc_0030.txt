For Alumni:
What are the Duke Benefits available to you?
- Access your profile on the Duke Alumni Network to search profiles of more than 190,000 alumni, view your giving history and make gifts.
- Join affinity, identity, interest, industry groups to get invited to events and programs.
- Update your address to receive information about regional programming around the world
- Download the Duke Alumni app for on the go access
- Tap into that famous Duke network when you Ask A Blue Devil
- Never stop learning and explore Duke’s Forever Learning lifelong learning resources, including online courses, webinars, reading lists, and educational excursions.
- Tap into that famous Duke network and boost your career!
- See which services you can still access from your career center or connect with your exclusive Duke Alumni Career Resources
- Consult a Duke Alumni Career Advisor
- Check out the full list of on and off campus benefits you can access
- Join the 41,000 strong Duke University Alumni Network on LinkedIn
- Learn more about undergraduate admissions at Duke through the Alumni Admission Program https://alumni.duke.edu/become-alumni-admissions-interviewer
- Nominate alumni who embody the Forever Duke spirit for Duke alumni awards
How can you support current Duke students?
- Support the Duke students and programs you feel most connected with by donating to one of Duke’s 16 annual funds!
- Become an alumni interviewer for prospective students
Learn how to become an interviewer
- Let us know if you’d like to speak on campus or meet students to share your post-Duke experiences
- Reach out to student groups or academic programs you were involved with at Duke. They are always looking to connect with and showcase their alumni!
- Update your profile on the Duke Alumni Network, especially your Professional information and your “Contact Me About” tags so students can reach out to you for informational interviews and advice.
For Students
How can you connect with alumni?
- Get matched via AI for a conversation or email exchange with alumni who share your interests when you Ask A Blue Devil
- Claim your profile on the Duke Alumni Network while you’re still a student, and join various affinity, interest, industry, and regional groups to get connected
- Sign up for Karsh Conversations and be among the first invited to dozens of campus programs where you can meet alumni
- Get matched via AI for a conversation or email exchange with alumni who share your interests when you Ask A Blue Devil
- Apply to serve on the Duke Student Alumni Board
- Stop by the Karsh Alumni and Visitors Center for a tour or to study in our beautiful lobby!
