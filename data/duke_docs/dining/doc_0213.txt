Root Causes' Fresh Produce Program
- Where: 902 N Mangum St., Durham, NC
- When: Saturdays, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- About: Every Saturday Morning from 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM, Root Causes, in addition to fullfilling deliveries of fresh produce to Durham community members identified as food insecure by their doctor, distributes free food and produce available on a first-come-first-served basis. The program does not collect information about program recipients and prioritizes healthy produce. Visit Root Causes if you are ever in need of free food and/or produce.

Urban Ministries of Durham Food Pantry
- Where: 410 Liberty Street, Durham, NC 27701
- When: Mondays, Wednesdays, & Fridays 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM; Tuesdays & Thursdays 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM & 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
- About: Urban Ministries of Durham (UMD) is the primary point of connection for those who are homeless, hungry, or in need in Durham. With the help of nearly 4,000 volunteers, countless donations from the community, and dedicated staff, UMD serves around 6,000 people annually. UMD is the primary public homeless shelter in Durham, and welcomes neighbors regardless of race, ethnicity, religious beliefs or non-faith, sexual orientation, gender identity, or disability. UMD provides immediate relief to those experiencing chronic or temporary homelessness and works with clients to end their homelessness as quickly and responsibly as possible.
