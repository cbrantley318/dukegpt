Duke University Library Hours - Perkins & Bostock Libraries

Regular Hours:
Monday - Thursday: 8:00 AM - 2:00 AM
Friday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Sunday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 AM

During Finals Week, Perkins Library is open 24 hours.
During Spring Break, hours are reduced to 9 AM - 5 PM Monday through Friday, closed weekends.

Lilly Library (East Campus):
Monday - Thursday: 8:00 AM - Midnight
Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: Noon - Midnight

Library Services:
- Interlibrary Loan (ILL): Request books/articles not in Duke's collection at library.duke.edu/ill
- Research consultations: Book a librarian at library.duke.edu/research/appointments
- Study rooms: Reserve online up to 7 days in advance at library.duke.edu/studyrooms
- Printing: Available at all libraries, $0.05/page black and white, $0.25/page color
