Duke University Dining Information

West Campus Dining Locations:

The Marketplace (West Union):
- Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
- Saturday-Sunday: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
- Swipes accepted, DukeCash accepted, credit cards accepted
- Dietary options: vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free stations available daily

The Loop (West Union):
- Monday-Friday: 7:30 AM - Midnight
- Saturday-Sunday: 10:00 AM - Midnight
- Late night option, DukeCash only after 9 PM

East Campus Dining:

East Campus Marketplace (Broadhead):
- Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Saturday-Sunday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Meal Plan Options:
- Block 150: 150 meals per semester + $200 DukeCash
- Block 225: 225 meals per semester + $150 DukeCash
- Unlimited: Unlimited swipes + $100 DukeCash
- All first-year students living on East Campus are required to have a meal plan.

DukeCash can be added online at cashnet.duke.edu or at the card office in the Bryan Center.

Duke Student Health:
Student Health is located at 305 Trent Drive.
Hours: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM.
Appointments: 919-681-9355 or online at studentaffairs.duke.edu/studenthealth
After hours urgent care: Duke Urgent Care at Pickett Road, open until 8 PM daily.
Counseling & Psychological Services (CAPS): 919-660-1000, available 24/7 for crisis support.
