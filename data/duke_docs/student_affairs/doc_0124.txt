Duke offers two distinct summer options for undergraduate students: Duke Summer Session and Duke Summer Experiences. Each program offers unique opportunities for undergraduate students. On-campus housing is available for both options.
PLEASE NOTE: Students are NOT required to live on campus if enrolled in summer courses or summer experiences.
Summer housing is for students who need housing during the summer session (May 12 - August 11). Applications will be approved for the following reasons:
- Enrolled in Summer Session 1 class(es)
- Enrolled in Summer Session 2 class(es)
- Enrolled in Summer Sessions 1 & 2 classes
- International student
- DKU
- +Program (Applied Ethics+, Arts+, Climate+, Code+, CS+, Data+, Doc+, History+, Math+)
The daily rate per person is:
- Single: $58.73, approx. $2,643 per session
- Double: $44.42, approx. $1,999 per session
Students approved to stay in summer housing will be housed in either Edens or Keohane based upon their program participation.
Housing Dates
Session 1 Move In = Tuesday, May 12
Session 1 Move Out = Friday, June 26
Session 2 Move In = Sunday, June 28
Session 2 Move Out = Tuesday, August 11
Extension housing is available for currently assigned residential students who need to stay on campus beyond the 12:00 PM move-out deadline on Monday, May 4.
Submitting an extension request does not guarantee approval. Requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis starting in mid-April, and students will be notified of their status within three days of submission.
If approved, you will be charged a daily rate and you may be required to relocate to a different on-campus assignment.
The application is available through Housing Cloud For questions, please email housing@duke.edu.
Extension Dates: May 4-May 11.
