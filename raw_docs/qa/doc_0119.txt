Q: What dining halls are on West Campus?
A: The main dining location on West Campus is The Marketplace in the Bryan Center, which is an all-you-can-eat dining hall open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The Brodhead Center (near East-West bus stop) has multiple restaurant-style options including Saladelia, Farmstead, Beyu Caffe, and others. There are also several coffee shops and cafes across campus.
