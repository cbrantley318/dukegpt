Did you know? You'll submit a new aid application each year you're at Duke, with updated income and asset information. If your family's financial circumstances remain similar year to year, you can expect your aid to be similar as well.
How It Works
November 3: Early Decision Deadline
Complete your CSS Profile and FAFSA by November 3rd, and keep an eye on your applicant portal to see if we request any additional documents. Please submit any additional requested documents promptly. We'll provide your aid offer with the offer of admission.
February 1: Regular Decision Deadline
Complete your FAFSA and CSS Profile by February 1, and keep an eye on your applicant portal to see if we request any additional documents. Please submit any additional requested documents promptly!
If your aid application isn't complete by the deadline, don't worry--your total eligibility for aid won't change.
We'll keep processing financial aid applications as they come in, however, so please submit your applications as soon as you can to receive your aid offer with your offer of admission.
