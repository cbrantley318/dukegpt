Studying Away
You can study across the globe and use your Duke financial aid! Study away programs are offered by Duke ("Duke-In") or by another university ("Duke-Approved"), and it's helpful to know how financial aid differs between the two types of programs. Read on to learn more about how financial aid can make these exciting opportunities a reality for you.
You can use your financial aid at Duke to take advantage of new opportunities year-round and across the globe!
