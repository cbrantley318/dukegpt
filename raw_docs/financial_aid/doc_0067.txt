Study Away
The study-away experience can be life-changing, and we want these extraordinary opportunities to be available to students regardless of their financial circumstances. You can take your financial aid with you across the globe!
There are two kinds of academic-year study away programs: Duke-In programs, which are administered by Duke directly, and Duke-Approved programs, which are administered by another university but are approved for Duke credit. Financial aid works a bit differently between these two kinds of programs, and you can read on below to learn more.
